Questions before you decide

Frequently asked

How do you detect churn risk if Skool has no API?
Skool exposes two triggers — new paid member and membership questions. We build the signal layer outside Skool. Every resource you share, every link you send via email, every event you run — members interact with these outside the Skool platform. We track those interactions and build an engagement score from them. No unofficial API, no scraping, no ToS risk.
How early do you actually catch churn?
Typically 7–14 days before cancellation. Members who are about to cancel show a clear pattern — they stop engaging with emails, stop clicking links, stop showing up to events. The system detects that decline in real time. By the time the automated re-engagement sequence fires, they haven't made a final decision yet. That's the window.
What do the re-engagement sequences actually say?
Level 1 is a soft check-in — personal, warm, no pressure. "Hey, noticed you haven't been around lately — everything okay?" Level 2 is a direct personal DM template that references what they originally joined for. Level 3 is a pause offer or bonus content drop — gives them a reason to stay without a hard sell. We write and load all three for your specific community voice before go-live.
Does the upgrade identification work for any backend offer?
Yes — coaching programs, masterminds, done-for-you services, course upgrades, annual plans, or any other offer you want to move warm members toward. We configure the invitation sequence to match your specific offer and desired action. Members who hit the engagement threshold get a personalized invitation timed to their peak interest.
What do I actually have to do?
Give us your API key and 30 minutes on a kickoff call. We build, test, and deploy everything. After that: share your resources through the tracking links we give you and check your dashboard once a week. Your ongoing time commitment is about 30 minutes per week — reviewing alerts and approving any manual interventions you want to make.
What happens if I cancel the subscription?
The system belongs to you. All Airtable bases, Zapier flows, GHL sequences, and dashboards live in your accounts. Cancel the subscription and the system keeps running — you just lose the monthly recalibration and support. No lock-in, no data held hostage, no exit fee.
How is this different from just sending more emails to my members?
Sending more emails to everyone is the reason churn stays high — people who aren't engaged don't respond to volume, they unsubscribe or go further quiet. Our system sends the right message to the right person at the right moment — based on their actual behavior, not a broadcast schedule. The re-engagement works because it's targeted and timed. Not because it's loud.
